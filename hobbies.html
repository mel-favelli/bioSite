Hobbies

David has a ton of hobbies and different ways he likes to spend his time. For work, David is a carpenter. He also occasionally does other remodeling jobs, too, like interior and exterior painting. Together, we have painted every single room in our house a different color. We will get to paint some more when it's time to move out because it's a rental, so we'll have to change everything back to white. David says it's not a waste of time because we're spending time and making memories inside of those walls - and who wants to be surrounded by plain, white walls? When David's not woodworking in the backyard or painting a neighbors house down the road, he enjoys riding bikes and taking Juneau and Bear for long walks at the many dog parks near his home.
